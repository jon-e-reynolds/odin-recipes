Grilled Cheese

Fast Lunch for the Sick

Pair it with tomato soup for maximum recovery

Ingredients

  • 1 package of cheese
  • 1 loaf of sliced bread
  • 1 container of butter

Steps

  1. Butter both sides of 2 pieces of bread
  2. Add pan to stove on medium heat
  3. Put slice of cheese between two pieces of buttered bread
  4. Add to pan cook to browned and then flip
