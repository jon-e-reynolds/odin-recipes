Lasagna

World's Best Lasagna

It takes a little work, but it is worth it.

Ingredients

  • 1lb italian sausage
  • 3/4lb lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 2 cans canned tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 12 lasagna noodes

Steps

  1. Cook meat over medium heat, add tomatoes and water
  2. Bring water to boil cook noodles for 10 minutes
  3. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  4. Spread meat sauce in pan, layer noodles, add cheese, repeat
  5. Bake in oven for 25 minutes
